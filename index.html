College Insights

Adaptiveness

Changes are inevitable so you have to be prepared!

I realized college is survival. There are times you'll feel happy and you're on top of the world, and there are times that you'll hit rock bottom. One thing that I can give advice is to focus on yourself and, at the same time, be a team player because helping each other is a lot better than doing things on your own.

01

Make Mistakes

It's okay to make mistakes as long as you learn from it

Just remember that we are not perfect, yet, we always strive to be. There will always be times where we will make mistakes and it is up to us if we continue those mistakes over and over again. It's better to make mistakes as early as possible in life or in your career because eventually, the stakes will be higher and heavier, which brings more responsibility and accountability. Learn from mistakes and failures to be a better person in the future!

02

Enjoy

The purpose of our lives is to be happy!

Be yourself. Express yourself. Enjoy every moment. Do what makes you happy and explore life! We, Filipinos, are good at finding happiness even when the situation is bad. We always squeeze the enjoyment and laughter in what we do and I think that is a trait that I'm proud of as a Filipino. Have a mindset of "they don't care about you, they care about themselves", and eventually you'll be contended with what you have. I may not live my college life as fulfulling as it should be, but I'm contended with it, and for me, I enjoyed every part of it!
